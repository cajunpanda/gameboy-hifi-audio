Switch between Full mode (all the sound processing) and Battery saver (low power, longer playtime) by holding the R button on the console until the third chime, then releasing. This page can't switch it, because battery saver puts the radio to sleep.

R-button hold menu

Hold the console's R button; it chimes as the hold passes each step. Release on the chime you want. Raise these if R gets bumped during play.
