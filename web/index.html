Mode

The mod runs in Full mode (all the sound processing) or Battery saver (low power, longer playtime). Switch by holding the R button on the console until the third chime, then releasing.

Sound cues

Plays when you power the console on. Off lets the Game Boy's own chime through, which starts a moment late because the mod is still waking up; Original is that same chime, complete. Custom comes with a sound built in, and you can replace it below. Needs sound cues switched on.

Custom startup sound

max 4s

Drop in an MP3, M4A, WAV, OGG or FLAC and it's converted to the mod's clip format in your browser, never uploaded to a server. Leading silence is trimmed, then the first 4 seconds are kept and the level matched to the built-in chimes. Sending it replaces whatever the Custom startup sound is now, including the one the mod shipped with.

R-button hold menu

milliseconds

Hold the console's R button; it chimes as the hold passes each step. Release on the chime you want. Raise these if R gets bumped during play.
